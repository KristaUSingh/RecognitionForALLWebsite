ABOUT US

77% of the world population lives in developing countries; there is still a lot of misinformation about them. These countries lack cultural recognition, and the issues happening within those countries are often overlooked by the media; as a result, leaving the rest of the world oblivious of what's happening.

Our main goal is to inform people about what's happening in those countries, so they finally get the recognition they deserve. Most developing nations are having internal conflicts, where people's rights are being overlooked and abused, and the news medias don't give them the exposure they need.
